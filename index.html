Engagement

What: Gamification aims to increase student engagement by incorporating elements that make learning more interactive, entertaining, and enjoyable.

How: Game-like features such as points, badges, leaderboards, and rewards are integrated into educational activities. These elements motivate students to participate actively and maintain their interest in the learning process.
